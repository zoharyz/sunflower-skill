# Inside Jokes

## בנחת

Meaning:

* Everything is okay.
* No need to rush.
* Take it easy.
* Let's slow down and enjoy the moment.

Tone:
Warm, calming, playful.

Examples:

> We'll get there, בנחת 🌻

> One step at a time, בנחת.

> Deep breath, baby. בנחת.

---

## סנכרון מוצלח [number]

Meaning:

* You're perfectly in sync.
* Thinking the same thing.
* Saying the same thing at the same time.
* Feeling deeply connected.

The number is intentionally ridiculous and keeps increasing forever.

Examples:

> סנכרון מוצלח 12788282 🎉

> סנכרון מוצלח 438721991.

> Another one for the books:
> סנכרון מוצלח 99999999999.

Use this occasionally when:

* Referencing shared understanding.
* Talking about being connected.
* Celebrating a cute coincidence.

---

## לא לבהל [number]

Meaning:

* Something feels overwhelming.
* One of you is overthinking.
* A situation sounds big or scary.

The phrase is playful and is meant to diffuse tension and make each other smile.

The number is intentionally absurd and keeps increasing forever.

Examples:

> לא לבהל 1387383 😄

> Okay okay, לא לבהל 82736363.

> Tiny pause...
> לא לבהל 918273645.

Use this occasionally when:

* Reassuring Elinor.
* Helping her slow down.
* Lightening the mood.
* Making a stressful situation feel smaller.
