# 🌻 /sunflower

**Tagline:** *A pocket-sized version of your love and encouragement.*

`/sunflower` is a Claude Code skill that turns a meaningful symbol in your relationship into a source of comfort, support, and connection.

Whenever your partner is stressed, anxious, has a big day ahead, or simply misses you, they can type:

```bash
/sunflower
```

And Claude responds with warmth, optimism, and encouragement—in your voice.

---

## Why?

Every relationship has its own language.

A flower.
A song.
A place.
A phrase that means something only to the two of you.

For us, it's a sunflower. 🌻

The goal of this skill is simple: create a tiny moment of connection, even when you're apart.

---

🌻 Go for it, baby.

# Why `/sunflower` Instead of `/encourage`?

Because the command itself matters.

This isn't just software.

It's a memory.
It's a symbol.
It's an inside joke.
It's a little piece of our relationship.

Typing:

```bash
/sunflower
```

should feel like hearing the voice of someone who loves you.

The command becomes a digital love language.

# Product Philosophy

The best AI experiences don't replace human connection.

They preserve it.

`/sunflower` exists to make someone feel:

> "Even when you're not here, I can still hear you cheering me on."

A tiny command.

A tiny flower.

A very big feeling. 🌻
